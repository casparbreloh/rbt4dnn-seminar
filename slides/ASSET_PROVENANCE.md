subject to the repository-wide exclusions in [`../NOTICE.md`](../NOTICE.md). Most visuals are
inline HTML/CSS/SVG reconstructions from committed tabular results. Three original paper tables are
cropped and reproduced under the paper's CC BY 4.0 license because their example images are the
evidence being discussed; course-slide imagery is not copied.

## Public artifact samples in the live deck

The live presentation hot-links a small, cited sample directly from the authors' public
artifact at the pinned commit recorded in `data/manifest.json`. The images are not copied
into this repository or the GitHub Pages artifact because the upstream repository does not
declare a redistribution license. Except for the three cited evidence crops above, paper
tables and diagrams are redrawn as native HTML for legibility.

`rbt4dnn-presentation.pdf` is a publication-safe export. The export script renders the deck,
replaces the upstream sample regions with source-linked placeholders, and assembles a flattened
PDF that retains only visual assets authored by this seminar project. The export is reproducible
with `scripts/export_presentation_pdf.sh`.

## Deliberately excluded material

- TUM marks and course-slide images: not republished.
- Full paper pages: only the three evidence-table crops listed above are reproduced, with CC BY 4.0
  attribution; other diagrams and tables remain native HTML reconstructions.
- Images copied from the upstream RBT4DNN artifact: not republished; reviewers can restore the
  pinned corpus through `scripts/fetch_data.py`.
- Dataset photographs and examples: not copied into the deck.

## Data-backed inline visuals

The deck's replication, validity, cost, shared-generator, and LLM-audit graphics are authored as
inline HTML/CSS/SVG. Values come from committed CSV files under `experiments/`; the slide captions
identify the relevant experiment and limitations. See the final slide for the original paper and
artifact links.
